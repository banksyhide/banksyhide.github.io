Our Team

Our Team!

Your Modern Accountant, is a small business headed up by Patrick Payne and supported by Grace Wong, Eda Voykhanskaya, and Nicole Jennex. During the busy tax season we bring on new team members to help with the day-to-day and personal tax returns. Patrick personally trains all of them, and we only pick from individuals who have graduated post-secondary in Accounting in Canada. So every team member who has worked or will work with us, are all considered accountants.

 
Patrick Payne
 

Patrick started this company back in 2010. From 2010 - May 2017 it was known as KHFinancial. Patrick started this company while he was in University at Ryerson for Accounting, as well as, a working full-time job. To this day, Patrick is the one who answers most emails, all of the calls (when he is between appointments) and does the lion's share of the work. Patrick is YMA, he takes a personal interest on all of the clients he takes on, and personally helps clean up back tax situations. He has big plans for YMA, and is working towards making tax filing in Canada easy, accessible, affordable and stress-free.

 
Eda Voykhanskaya
 

Eda Voykhanskaya has been with YMA since December 2017. She is on her way through the CPA program and has proven a valuable asset to our company. Eda works directly with our self-employed and corporate clients to ensure all of their needs are met

 
Nicole Jennex
 

Nicole Jennex has been with YMA since January 2018. She specializes in standard and self-emplpoyed tax filing. She works closely with our clients to assit them with their taxes, as well as, CRA needs and communication. She is key in our requests, objections and adjustments with the CRA that we make on behalf of our clients.

 
Our Past Tax Heroes
 

As tax filing in Canada is seasonal, during January until about June we bring on a few extra team members to help us out. Patrick personally trains them on all internal software and processes, as well as, customer serivce. All previous and new Tax Heroes have a post-secondary education in Accounting and/or Taxation in Canada from a reputable University or College.
