Frequent Questions

 

We know that many people have questions when it comes to financial information. How do I do this? and What will happen if I do this? As well as questions about what we at YMA do. Here is a quick list of some of those questions. If you have a question that's not answered here, please feel free to contact us and we'll provide you with an answer ASAP.


The Information we require can be different for each person. See our list on our Downloads Page for possible documents that may apply to you. If you have a question about whether you need a certain document, feel free to contact us.

Your records should contain clear documentation of all transactions that have transpired with your company. Sketchy or incomplete records that use approximates instead of exact amounts are not acceptable by CRA.

Documentation that is not complete, can lead to CRA disallowing expenses.

We offer a variety of services to both individuals and businesses. Please follow either the personal link of Business link in our menu bar for specifics. If there is a service you need but do not see it in our list, please contact us with your inquiry.

Our basic fees are listed on our rates page. But please feel free to contact us for things accurate rate.

We charge both ways, depending on the service we are offering and what our client prefers.

For on-site bookkeeping, one of our bookkeepers goes to work in your office, at your desk and on your computers. For off-site bookkeeping, we pick up documents from you and process them in our office and return them during our next visit.

We value privacy and confidentiality. We will not be giving out your information to any third party unless requested by you in writing. We also back-up and encrypt any information regarding your records that we maintain, to redundant drives to avoid the possibility of deletion.

When you hire someone to come into your office to do the work you have: office space expenses, additional equipment expenses, payroll expenses, benefits expenses and tax payments. All of these expenses add up over time. When you use our services, you eliminate most of these expenses because the employment, office and equipment expenses are paid by us.

You can start by contacting us via; email, instant messenger (G-Talk) or phone.

We use Wave Accounting, Xero Accounting, Kashoo Accounting and Quickbooks Online for bookkeeping and accounting. For tax filing we use My Tax Express. We also use HubDoc, Google Drive, Evernote, Dropbox, and Onedrive
