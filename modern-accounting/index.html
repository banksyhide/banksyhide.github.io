Things To Know About Your Modern Accountant
In case you didn't know, we are a tiny company, only 4 people strong right now. Answering the phone is sometimes hard to do as we are in and out of meetings all day.

If you call and don't hear back from us after a few days, please don't get offended, we are trying our best. The best way to get a hold of someone is thorugh email. We are trying to help as many people as we can with their taxes and we only have so much capacity. Because we don't hold a regular office, ALL services we offer are either by appointment or done remotely, which for certain tax filings comes with a 15% discount. For people looking for tax filing where they are employed and only have 1 year of filing to do, we can only take this on remotely, which qualifies for the discount.
