All About Filing Taxes Remotely

What it Means to File Your Taxes Online

 

The most important thing to note about remote tax filing, or any cloud work we do, is that a real, educated, Canadian is doing the work at all times. Nothing we do goes offshore, it is all done in Canada by professional with the highest standards.

Benefits of Working Remotely


There are a large number of benefits from working via the internet in a secure way.
Here’s a list of the main reasons our clients choose a remote solution over anything else.

  • Saves time – Not everyone has time to schedule an appointment, and sit down for an hour or two to go over everything. Being the 21st century, people are moving faster than ever before and even though taxes are very important, they usually get bumped down the list. Working remotely means you can take 10 minutes to get us your tax documents and then respond to questions when it works for you.
  • Access anywhere – No need to be in the same city or same country to work with great professionals. Whether you’re traveling, or just can’t find anyone in your city you like, we’re available cross Canada to prepare all Canadian returns, except Quebec returns. Sorry QB!
  • Stress-free – Knowing someone else who is educated, trustworthy and looking out for you is working with you, leaves you feeling a sense of relief. Let us worry about your taxes!
  • Saves money – That’s right! Filing remotely saves you money! How? Simple, it saves us money so we pass that savings directly on to you! We offer a 15% discount on all remotely filed tax returns. *Sorry, the discount doesn’t apply to student returns, already really cheap, or corporate returns.


With our streamlined and secure remote tax filing process, you are connected with a dedicate Tax Hero who you can ask questions at any time via email or instant chat through Google Hangouts. Our aim is to make tax filing as painless and stress-free as possible, and we think that our remote filing solution as achieved that.

Though we think we’re great at what we do, we are always modifying our processes to make them better and easier for you, so if you have any suggestions, feedback or ideas, please let us know!

Contact us by phone or email with any questions you may have to get your taxes filed from ANYWHERE.
